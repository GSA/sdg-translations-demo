    تعريفات ESA: Employment and Support Allowance; CVM: Chained Volume Measure; CRA: Country and Regional Analysis. This indicator uses the Classification of the Functions of Government (COFOG) codes 07 (Health), 09 (Education) and 10 (Social protection).
