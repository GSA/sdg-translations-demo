    可用指标 Percentage of total government spending on essential services (education, health and social protection)
    指标描述
    地域覆盖范围 United Kingdom
    计量单位 Percentage (%)
    定义 ESA: Employment and Support Allowance; CVM: Chained Volume Measure; CRA: Country and Regional Analysis. This indicator uses the Classification of the Functions of Government (COFOG) codes 07 (Health), 09 (Education) and 10 (Social protection).
    计算 % of National Budget = (ESA expenditure (£m) / National Budget (£m)) * 100 OR % of Gross Domestic Product = (ESA expenditure (£m) / GDP (£m)) * 100
