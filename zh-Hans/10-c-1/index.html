    可用指标 Remittance costs as a percentage of the amount remitted
    指标描述
    地域覆盖范围 United Kingdom
    计量单位 Percentage (%)
    定义 This indicator provides data on the cost of sending and receiving small amounts of money from one country to another. Called remittances, these international transfers are often initiated by migrant workers.
    计算 Average Total Cost is calculated as the average total cost for sending USD 200 with all Remittance Service Providers worldwide.
    其他资料 Remittance Prices Worldwide methodology
