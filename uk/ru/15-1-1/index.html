    Единица измерения Percentage (%)
    Определения Woodland certification assesses management practices against agreed environmental standards. Certification requires that wood products are harvested legally and sustainably, and that important wildlife habitats are identified and are not negatively impacted by management.
    Расчёты (Area of woodland / Area of land mass ) * 100 OR (area of certified woodland / Area of land mass ) * 100
