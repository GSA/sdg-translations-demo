    Название показателя Proportion of people living below 50 per cent of median income, by sex, age and persons with disabilities
    Название задачи By 2030, empower and promote the social, economic and political inclusion of all, irrespective of age, sex, disability, race, ethnicity, origin, religion or economic or other status.
    Описание глобального показателя The indicator is calculated as the proportion of persons living in households (adjusted for household size) below 60% of the national median income, using population-weighted subgroup estimates from household surveys.
